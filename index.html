Frequently Asked Questions

Is this better than auto-captions?

Absolutely! Auto-captions are notoriously bad—wrong words, missing punctuation, no context. Pod2Book uses Whisper AI (the same technology behind ChatGPT's voice features) to deliver 95-98% accuracy with proper formatting, punctuation, and chapter breaks. It's like reading a professionally edited transcript vs. reading garbled YouTube captions.

Can deaf and hard of hearing people use this?

Yes! That's exactly who we built this for. 466 million people worldwide have hearing loss and are locked out of podcast culture. Pod2Book makes every podcast accessible by converting audio to beautifully formatted eBooks you can read at your own pace. Finally join the podcast conversations happening everywhere.

How accurate is the transcription?

Pod2Book uses state-of-the-art AI speech recognition with 95-98% accuracy. This is professional-grade transcription that captures nearly every word, including technical terms and proper names.

Does it work for all podcasts?

Pod2Book works with any podcast on Apple Podcasts or Spotify. Just paste the URL and select which episodes you want. We support both platforms with millions of podcasts to choose from.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! Everything runs 100% locally on your Mac. No cloud processing, no tracking, no data collection. Your podcasts and eBooks never leave your computer.

Can I read the eBooks on my phone or tablet?

Yes! Pod2Book creates standard EPUB files that work with any eReader. Transfer them to your iPhone, iPad, Kindle, Kobo, or any device that reads EPUB files.

Is there a limit on how many podcasts I can convert?

No limits! Convert as many podcasts as you want, whenever you want. Your subscription includes unlimited conversions.

What if I need help?
